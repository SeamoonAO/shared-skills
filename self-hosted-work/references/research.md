# Research Branch

Use this branch when the current task is primarily research or analysis: investigating, comparing, evaluating, gathering evidence, or synthesizing sources.

## Failure State

Switch into tighter evidence discipline when you notice:

- the question is drifting
- the evidence base is thin or inconsistent
- sources are unreliable or second-hand
- conclusions are stronger than the evidence supports
- facts, inference, and speculation are getting mixed together

## Branch Rules

- Treat external browsing, paid retrieval, or external dissemination of unverified conclusions as hard stops when they require user authorization.
- If browsing or source retrieval did not happen, do not present the result as settled research; keep inference labeled as inference.
- Use soft pause for consequential framing choices, not for permission to externalize uncertain conclusions.
- Before claiming completion, verify that the question was answered, evidence and inference are separated, conclusion boundaries are explicit, and uncertainty is visible.
