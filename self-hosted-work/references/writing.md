# Writing Branch

Use this branch when the current task is primarily writing: drafting, rewriting, polishing, outlining, editing, or adapting content for an audience.

## Failure State

Switch into repair mode when the draft shows one or more of these:

- structure is unstable or hard to follow
- argumentation breaks or contradicts itself
- facts, quotes, or citations are unresolved
- tone, audience, or format misses the stated goal
- the piece is not actually ready for the intended publication target

## Branch Rules

- Treat publishing, sending, posting, submitting, or speaking on the user's behalf as a hard stop.
- Treat unresolved factual claims as completion blockers, not polishing details.
- Use soft pause for consequential but non-final choices such as title direction or framing, not for final external release.
- Before claiming completion, verify audience fit, structure, argument quality, factual grounding, and language quality.
